Maa Bhangayani Temple

The temple lies at an altitude of 8000 feet above sea level. God sister of Shirgul Mahadev, Maa bhangayni is said to be the most powerful deity worshipped in Sirmour, Himachal Pradesh. This temple has a history that connects Maa Bhangayni to Chureshwar Mahadev (Churdhar).

Quick facts:

Haripurdhar is a small town in Sirmour, Himachal Pradesh, India. The town is situated on a high ridge overlooking a deep valley at an altitude of 2500 metres above sea level. It was earlier known as 'Dungbhangayani' and was the summer capital of Sirmour.
